# cassette-player-plus
Neoforge 1.21.1 1.21.X

Turn any music disc into a portable cassette and take your soundtrack with you. This mod lets you craft cassettes from any music disc — vanilla or modded — and play them through a handheld cassette player.

Just right‑click the cassette player to open it, insert your cassette, and enjoy your music anywhere in the world.

Features

Convert any music disc into a cassette

Fully compatible with modded discs

Portable cassette player you can use anywhere

Simple right‑click interface

Survival‑friendly crafting

Perfect for players who want their own soundtrack on the go.
